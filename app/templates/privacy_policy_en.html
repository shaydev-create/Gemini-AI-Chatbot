Gemini AI Futuristic Chatbot Chrome Extension

Last Updated: October 10, 2025
TL;DR: We respect your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share your personal data. All conversations are processed locally or through encrypted connections to Google's Gemini API.

1. Information We Collect

Our Chrome extension prioritizes your privacy and collects minimal information:

  • No Personal Identification: We do not collect names, emails, or personal identifiers
  • Chat Messages: Stored locally in your browser only (never sent to our servers)
  • API Key: Stored locally and encrypted in your browser storage
  • Settings: User preferences stored locally in Chrome storage
  • Usage Analytics: We do not track usage or collect analytics

2. How We Use Information

The limited data we process is used solely for:

  • Providing AI chat functionality through Google Gemini API
  • Saving your conversation history locally for your convenience
  • Maintaining your settings and preferences
  • Ensuring the extension works properly

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All your data stays on your device
  • Encryption: API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your data on external servers
  • Easy Deletion: Clear extension data anytime from Chrome settings

4. Third-Party Services

This extension integrates with:

  • Google Gemini API: For AI-powered responses (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • No Other Third Parties: We do not share data with any other services

When using the Google Gemini API, your messages are sent to Google's servers for processing. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

5. Permissions Explanation

Our extension requests these permissions:

  • storage: To save your settings and conversation history locally
  • activeTab: To interact with the current webpage when needed
  • scripting: To inject necessary scripts for functionality
  • declarativeNetRequest: For network request handling
  • offscreen: For background processing
  • host_permissions: To communicate with Google Gemini API

6. Data Retention

  • Chat history is stored locally until you manually delete it
  • Settings persist until you uninstall the extension
  • No automatic data expiration (you control your data)
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data

7. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data through the extension interface
  • Export: Download your conversation history as text files
  • Delete: Clear individual conversations or all data
  • Modify: Edit or update your settings anytime

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has used this extension, please contact us to remove any data.

9. International Users (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Union:

  • We process data based on legitimate interest for functionality
  • You have the right to access, rectify, and erase your data
  • Data processing occurs locally on your device
  • You can object to data processing by uninstalling the extension

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Updates will be posted on our GitHub repository and the Chrome Web Store listing.
